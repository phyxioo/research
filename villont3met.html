Materials and Equipments:
Loam Soil, Pechay Seeds, 2 Small Rectangular Plastic Container, and Water

Procedures:
Prepare the container, soil and the seeds, Put soil in the 2 containers and make sure they are evenly the same amount, Distribute 8 seeds in the 2 containers where the soil is, After distributing the seeds and covering them up with soil, water both the container, Wherein, Plant A is watered with the amount of 20 mL while Plant B is with the amount of 30 mL, and Leave it out in the sun and water them everyday.

Variables:
Independent Variable: Amount of Water Dependent Variable: Growth of the Pechay Constant: Amount of Sunlight
