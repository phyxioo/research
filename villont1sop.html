This research aims to address the feasibility and effectiveness of creating an organic candle using natural ingredients. Specifically, the researchers seek answers to the following questions:

1.) How can we formulate an organic candle using eco-friendly materials?
2.) What are the benefits of using organic ingredients in candle-making?
3.) How does the performance of an organic candle compare to conventional commercial candles in terms of burn time, scent, and durability?
