RESEARCH DESIGN

Using a non-experimental quantitative design, this study evaluates the effectiveness of low-cost materials like Moringa seeds and pebbles in reducing wastewater turbidity through controlled, replicated laboratory filtration trials.

PARTICIPANTS
Marikina City residents will evaluate the performance of Moringa-based versus standard water filters by observing wastewater filtration and providing feedback via a Likert scale survey to determine the more effective system.

MATERIALS AND EQUIPMENTS
This research utilizes Moringa oleifera seeds, mineral beads, and standard filters to treat wastewater, using a pH meter, scale, and mortar and pestle to measure and prepare the materials for comparative testing.
